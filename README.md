## How to Use These Blueprints

Each blueprint includes:

1. **Problem Statement** — What manual work the role currently does and why it's painful
2. **Workflow Architecture** — Visual diagram of the automated pipeline
3. **Implementation Code** — Python scripts, AI prompts, and configuration examples
4. **Example Output** — A realistic sample of what the automation produces
5. **Setup Guide** — Prerequisites, timeline, and cost estimates
6. **ROI Analysis** — Time saved, cost savings, and risk reduction metrics

These blueprints are designed for non-technical individuals and small businesses looking to incorporate AI into their daily operations.
